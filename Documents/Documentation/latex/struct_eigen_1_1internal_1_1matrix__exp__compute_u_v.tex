Compute the (17,17)-\/\+Pad\'{e} approximant to the exponential. 

After exit, $ (V+U)(V-U)^{-1} $ is the Pad\'{e} approximant of $ \exp(A) $ around $ A = 0 $.

This function activates only if your long double is double-\/double or quadruple.
